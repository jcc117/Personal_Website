About Me

I was raised in the Pittsburgh suburb of South Park, where I excelled in technology, mathematics, and engineering. After graduating in 2014 as the valedictorian of my class I began attending the University of Pittsburgh, majoring in electrical engineering. I switched my major to computer science and Japanese language and culture after one semester. I excelled in more coures and became proficient in multiple programming languages. My core interests include web applications, operatings systems, and compilers.

I have also participated in multilple extra-curricular activities as well. The first of which was joining the University of Pittsburgh Varisty Marching Band as a saxophone player. I am also a member of Tau Beta Sigma, an honorary band sorority that serves the Pitt Band. As a junior I held the position of chapter historian and was elected as a member of the executive board my senior year.
